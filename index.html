Manifesto
(A work in progress!)

No more deaths. No more danger. No more toxic air. The time is over for compromises that leave the car as the dominate feature of our city scape. Lets not be afraid to demand a city that is radically safe, clean and healthy for everyone, and where:

Business districts are free of motorized vehicles * Fifty percent of each street is dedicated to non­motorized traffic * Bike lanes are wide enough for two riders abreast * Resident parking fees equal the cost of an average bike * All major roadways include dedicated bus lanes * Half of all parking lots and parking garages are repurposed as parks and housing * Motorist are fully liable for all accidents involving a cyclist or pedestrian * Residents have the right to convert the road space abutting their property to a garden plot, community enrichment project or privy * Motorists are fined for any non­emergency use of a horn * Streets are safe for all ages * Motorized vehicles are limited to average speed of bicycles * A minimum five foot separation exists for all motorized traffic * Parking is prohibited within 50 feet of all intersections * All residential streets are posted, no through traffic * Cars are required to post signage indicating their public cost ex.I pollute and use oil harvested through the loss of human life
